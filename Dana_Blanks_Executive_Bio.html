About

Born in Wilmington and raised in Leland, Dana's understanding of the Cape Fear region is rooted in lived experience. Licensed in North Carolina, South Carolina, and Georgia, she has successfully expanded her firm's footprint across coastal North Carolina and South Carolina — and is now extending that same standard into North Carolina's Triangle region from a new base in Wake Forest.

Her team of nine advisors and three staff are positioned in key markets to deliver the local expertise that matters most to every client.

From Top Producer to Team Builder

Dana's multimillion-dollar sales record reflects her personal success in luxury homes and commercial land. Her legacy, however, is now built through the talented advisors she mentors — a team empowered to deliver exceptional results throughout North Carolina, South Carolina, and the Triangle.

As founder of a Woman & Minority Owned brokerage, Dana brings a diverse perspective to every transaction. While she focuses primarily on firm growth and leadership, she continues to represent a limited number of referral clients.
